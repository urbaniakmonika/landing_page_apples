Discover Polish Apples

Polish apples are known for their crisp texture and balanced flavors. From sweet to tart, they brighten up every meal and bring a taste of tradition to your table.

Apple life cycle in 4 steps

Blossoming

In spring, apple trees bloom with delicate flowers that attract pollinators.

Fruit Set

Bees transfer pollen, enabling flowers to develop into small green apples.

Growth

Through summer, apples grow bigger, ripen, and develop color and flavor.

Harvesting

In autumn, ripe apples are picked and prepared for eating or processing.

In the scent of a Polish apple lies the silence of the orchard, the warmth of summer sun, and a taste you remember from childhood. It's not just a fruit. It's history, tradition, and home.

-Polish Apple Grower
